Some would say 2020 was the worst time to change but for Leighton it was an opportunity to retool his toolbox and start pursuing passion over income. Tech has been a hobby of his since he was young. Much to his parents dismay he was often found taking apart electronic toys to get a peak at the inner workings from the age he could hold a screwdriver. When the household got its first computer his passion really took off. From that moment on he was either found roaming the farm or inside on the computer. The two polar opposite interests of outdoors and computers proved a unique challenge for Leighton when it came to picking a career path. After pursuing a career in the outdoors he decided his passion for the IT industry was stronger thus deciding to restudy.

After building multiple projects in his spare time Leighton decided that putting these projects out into the internet would be a mutually beneficial exercise. The internet would get more information in a fast developing industry and he would get to practice communicating his ideas to an audience, something he considers an area he could improve in. The website began as a way to understand HTML, CSS and JavaScript. He wanted to understand web design and the way data interacts with websites. From there it became a way to display projects on his journey to understanding specifics of IT systems.
